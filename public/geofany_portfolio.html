As part of handling 50+ support tickets per month at PT Pinetop Technology, a client reported an error on the TalentCloud.ai platform: "Cannot Exceed Yearly Limit" — despite their Flexi Benefit balance still being available. I investigated across multiple affected accounts, confirmed it as a system bug, documented the reproduction steps, and escalated to the programming team for resolution.
